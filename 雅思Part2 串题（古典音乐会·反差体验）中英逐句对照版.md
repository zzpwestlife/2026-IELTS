# 雅思Part2 串题（古典音乐会·反差体验）中英逐句对照版

### 话题18：不享受的音乐活动

 **中文** ：我参加过一场不享受的音乐活动，/是上个月和好朋友一起去的古典交响乐音乐会。/我们本来想尝试些高雅的事物，/加上朋友抢到了低价票，/就决定去体验一下。
     **英文** ：The music event I didn’t enjoy was a classical symphony concert /（中）I went to with my best friend last month. /（中）We wanted to try something elegant, /（中）and my friend got cheap tickets, /（强调）so we decided to give it a go.（中）

 **中文** ：音乐会在市中心的专业音乐厅举行，/乐团演奏技巧确实很娴熟，/但我全程提不起兴趣。/古典音乐节奏大多缓慢舒缓，/我对这类曲风不敏感，/听了半小时就开始犯困，/越往后越觉得枯燥，/只盼着演出快点结束。
     **英文** ：It was held in a professional concert hall in the city center. /（中）The orchestra played skillfully, /（转折）but I couldn’t get into it at all. /（中）Most classical music is slow and gentle, /（中）and I’m not into this style. /（快）I started feeling sleepy after half an hour, /（中）and it got more and more boring as time went on. /（慢）I just waited for the performance to end quickly.（中）

### 话题3：禁止用手机的场合

 **中文** ：我经历过的禁止用手机的场合，/就是那场古典音乐会。/时间是上个月周六晚上，/地点在市中心的那家专业音乐厅。/入场时工作人员逐一检查，/要求大家把手机放进密封袋寄存。
     **英文** ：The occasion where I wasn’t allowed to use my phone /（中）was that classical concert. /（中）It was on a Saturday night last month, /（中）at that professional concert hall downtown. /（快）Staff checked everyone at the entrance, /（中）asking us to put our phones into sealed bags for storage.（中）

 **中文** ：他们说这样是为了保持安静，/避免手机灯光、铃声干扰演奏和其他观众。/我本来想拍几张环境照留念，/还想和朋友吐槽无聊，/结果完全没法用手机。/刚开始很不适应，/后来也被迫专注于现场，/算是这个规定的小收获。
     **英文** ：They said it was to keep quiet, /（中）and avoid phone lights or rings disturbing the performance and other audiences. /（中）I originally wanted to take some photos as a souvenir, /（快）and complain about being bored with my friend, /（中）but I couldn’t use my phone at all. /（中）I felt uncomfortable at first, /（转折）but later I was forced to focus on the scene—/（强调）that was a small gain from this rule.（中）

### 话题4：微笑的场合

 **中文** ：让很多人同时微笑的场合，/就发生在那场音乐会结束后。/我和朋友正疲惫地起身离场，/现场气氛还很沉闷，/大家都低着头慢慢走，/没什么交流。 **英文** ：The occasion where many people smiled at the same time /（中）happened right after that concert. /（中）My friend and I stood up tiredly to leave. /（中）The atmosphere was still dull, /（中）everyone walked slowly with their heads down, /（快）and there was little communication.（中）

 **中文** ：负责疏导人群的工作人员见状，/突然模仿起交响乐里小提琴、圆号的声音，/夸张又滑稽的样子瞬间打破了安静。/他还笑着调侃大家坐得辛苦，/全场人都忍不住笑了，/沉闷的气氛一下子变得轻松温馨。
     **英文** ：Seeing this, the staff in charge of guiding the crowd /（快）suddenly imitated the sounds of violins and French horns in the symphony. /（中）His exaggerated and funny gestures broke the silence at once. /（中）He also joked with a smile that everyone must have sat tiredly. /（强调）Everyone couldn’t help laughing, /（中）and the dull atmosphere became relaxed and warm in a second.（中）
