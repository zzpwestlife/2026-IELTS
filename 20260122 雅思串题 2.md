# 【1-4新题】串题分组5

- 想拥有的科技产品
- 花费超过预期的物品

物品, 地点 2026.1-4 新题

# 串题思路分析

你可以将「想拥有的科技产品」和「花费超过预期的物品」合并，讲述你在某个商场看到一款心仪的科技产品 （如智能家居设备），并因其独特功能而决定购买。你可以描述在商场的购物体验，如何被产品吸引，最终决定花费超出预算的原因（如对生活质量的提升）。同时，通过提到这个商场的名字和位置，串联起「商场」的话题，展示你在这个地方的购物习惯和购买的其他物品。这样，你只需围绕同一个购物经历调整细节，就能自然覆盖这组话题。

# Tips

选一个“购物体验”作为素材，比如你在商场看到的智能家居设备， 描述你对它的期待和最终的购买决定。具体例子可以是：智能音响、智能灯光系统、家用机器人、VR设备、健康监测器。

## 11 想拥有的科技产品

Describe a piece of technology (not a phone) that you would like to own

You should say:

• What it is

• How much it costs

• How you knew it

• And explain why you would like to own it

◎思路提示

- 单反相机、无人机、游戏本、智能手表、电子阅读器

## 23 花费超过预期的物品

Describe an item on which you spent more than expected You should say:

• What it is

• How much you spent on it

• Why you bought it

• And explain why you think you spent more than expected

## 14 商场

Describe a shopping mall You should say:

• What its name is

• Where it is

• How often you visit it

• And what you usually buy at the mall

思路提示

# 英文答案

段落 1：通用开头段
I’m going to share a story about my experience with a tech product that I really wanted and how it led me to a shopping place I often visit. This story is all about my journey with the Apple Watch SE and my favorite mall, Wanda Plaza.

段落 2：（话题 11&23）想拥有的科技产品 & 花费超过预期的物品
So, the tech product I wanted was the Apple Watch SE (special edition). I first heard about it from my friends who use iPhones, and they kept telling me how great it was, especially for tracking fitness and staying connected. The official price was 2999 yuan, but I ended up spending 3098 yuan because I also bought an extra original strap for 99 yuan. I had initially planned to spend around 2000 yuan, but once I tried it on at the digital store in Wanda Plaza, I was sold. Besides, the watch not only tracks my heart rate and sleep but also syncs my workout data to my iPhone. For someone like me who loves running and hates missing messages, it was perfect. Plus, the original strap felt much nicer than the cheaper ones, so I decided to get it. Although I spent more than I planned, I think it was totally worth it.

段落 3：（话题 14）购物场所
Now, let me tell you about Wanda Plaza, the mall where I bought my Apple Watch. It’s located in the heart of my new neighborhood, just a five-minute walk from the subway station. I usually visit this mall about once or twice a week. Sometimes I go with friends on weekends to shop, and other times I stop by after work to pick up daily necessities. For example, I love going to the basement floor for bubble tea and trendy snacks, and I often shop at Uniqlo on the first floor for casual outfits. The digital store where I bought my Apple Watch is also on the first floor, and I often check out headphones and iPads there too. On the third floor, I enjoy dining with friends. This mall has everything I need, from shopping to dining and even relaxing, making it my go-to place for hanging out and shopping.

段落 4：通用结尾段
In conclusion, my experience with the Apple Watch SE and my visits to Wanda Plaza have been quite enjoyable. I feel that the watch has made my life easier and more organized, especially when it comes to my fitness and communication. Overall, I love that I have a place like Wanda Plaza where I can find everything I need, and it’s always a fun outing with friends.

# 适配说明

素材用法：
● 把故事当作“素材库”，不是“标准答案”
● 每次答题前快速判断：这个题最关注哪一部分？
● 再从故事中挑选出相关内容，重组段落，简单调整即可！

推荐做法：
● 不要每题都讲一模一样的内容，会让考官怀疑你背稿。
● 根据题目侧重点选取最相关的部分，其他段落可以删掉或简化。
● 用你自己的语言重新组织内容，不需要逐字背诵。

这个故事怎么用在不同题目里？
你现在有一个完整故事，但不能每个话题都用整篇内容。太长会跑题，而且容易被识别为串题。下面教你怎么拆分使用，灵活组合段落，把它变成多个话题答案。
