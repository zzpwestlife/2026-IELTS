## Group 2: 童年与旧物 (Childhood & Old Objects)

### 1. 话题详情 (Topic Details)

#### Topic 1: 家中老物件
> **Describe an old object in your family**
> You should say:
> - What it is
> - How long your family has kept it
> - Where it came from
> - And explain why it is important to your family

#### Topic 2: 童年玩具
> **Describe a toy you liked in your childhood**
> You should say:
> - What it was
> - Who gave it to you
> - How you played with it
> - And explain why you liked it

#### Topic 3: 弄坏的东西
> **Describe a time when you broke something**
> You should say:
> - What it was
> - How you broke it
> - What you did after you broke it
> - And explain how you felt about it

### 2. 核心串题思路
> **串联逻辑**：
> 讲述一个**传承的布娃娃 (Handmade Doll)** 或**玩具车**的故事。
> - **老物件/童年玩具**：这是一个妈妈亲手做给我的布娃娃，或者是爸爸送的复古玩具车，保存了很多年。
> - **弄坏东西**：小时候不小心把它弄坏了（比如扯断了胳膊或摔坏了轮子），当时非常伤心和愧疚，后来修好了，现在成为了珍贵的纪念。

### 3. 通用素材库：传承的布娃娃

**English Answer**

**(Opening)**
I’m going to talk about an **old rag doll** / that has been in my family for over 20 years. / It was also my favorite toy / when I was a child. //
我要讲一个在我家已经有20多年的旧布娃娃。它也是我小时候最喜欢的玩具。

**(Description)**
My mother made it for me / using some leftover fabric / when I was about five years old. / It’s not fancy, / but it has a smiling face / and wears a colorful floral dress. //
我妈妈在我大概五岁的时候，用一些剩下的布料给我做的。它并不豪华，但它有一张笑脸，穿着一件色彩鲜艳的花裙子。

**(The "Broken" Incident)**
I remember one time, / I was playing "doctor" with the doll / and I accidentally **pulled its arm off**. / I was devastated / and started crying immediately / because I thought I had ruined my precious friend. //
我记得有一次，我和娃娃玩“医生”游戏，不小心把它的胳膊扯掉了。我当时崩溃了，立马哭了起来，因为我以为我毁了我珍贵的朋友。

**(Resolution)**
My mom heard me crying / and came to check on me. / Instead of scolding me, / she patiently sewed the arm back on. / Now, that stitch is still visible, / reminding me of my childhood / and my mother's love. //
妈妈听到我的哭声来看看我。她没有责骂我，而是耐心地把胳膊缝了回去。现在，那个缝线仍然清晰可见，提醒着我的童年和妈妈的爱。
