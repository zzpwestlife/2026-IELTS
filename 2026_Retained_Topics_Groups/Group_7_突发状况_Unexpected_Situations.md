## Group 7: 突发状况 (Unexpected Situations)

### 1. 话题详情 (Topic Details)

#### Topic 1: 和亲友的晚餐
> **Describe a dinner you had with your friends or family**

#### Topic 2: 突然停电
> **Describe a time when the electricity went out**

#### Topic 3: 别人向你道歉
> **Describe a time when someone apologized to you**

### 2. 核心串题思路
> **串联逻辑**：
> 讲述一次**家庭聚餐 (Family Dinner)** 时的**停电 (Power Cut)** 插曲。
> - **晚餐**：那是除夕夜（或生日晚餐），全家人聚在一起吃火锅。
> - **停电**：突然家里停电了，四周一片漆黑。
> - **道歉**：原来是物业在维修电路时失误导致的，后来物业经理特意上门**道歉**并说明情况。
> - **结果**：我们点起了蜡烛，烛光晚餐反而更有氛围，大家聊得更开心了。

### 3. 通用素材库：烛光晚餐意外

**English Answer**

**(The Dinner)**
I recall a memorable dinner / I had with my family / on New Year's Eve. / We were enjoying a delicious **hot pot** / at home, / chatting and laughing. //
我回想起除夕夜和家人的一次难忘晚餐。我们在家里享受美味的火锅，有说有笑。

**(The Power Cut)**
Suddenly, / the lights flickered and went out. / The whole apartment / plunged into darkness. / It was a **power cut**. / At first, we were confused / and a bit annoyed / because the hot pot stopped boiling. //
突然，灯光闪烁并熄灭了。整个公寓陷入了黑暗。停电了。起初，我们感到困惑和有点恼火，因为火锅不煮了。

**(Apology & Resolution)**
Later, we found out it was a maintenance error. / The property manager came to our door / and **apologized** profusely / for the inconvenience. / While waiting for the power to come back, / we lit some candles. / It turned into a romantic **candlelight dinner**, / and we actually talked more deeply / without the distraction of TV or phones. //
后来我们发现是维修失误。物业经理来到我们家门口，为带来的不便连连道歉。在等电来的时候，我们点了一些蜡烛。这变成了一顿浪漫的烛光晚餐，没有电视和手机的干扰，我们实际上聊得更深入了。
