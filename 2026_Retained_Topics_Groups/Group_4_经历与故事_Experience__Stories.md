## Group 4: 经历与故事 (Experience & Stories)

### 1. 话题详情 (Topic Details)

#### Topic 1: 等待特别事情
> **Describe a time when you waited for something special to happen**

#### Topic 2: 兴奋活动
> **Describe an exciting activity that you experienced for the first time**

#### Topic 3: 外语沟通
> **Describe a time when you used a foreign language to communicate for the first time**

#### Topic 4: 传统故事
> **Describe a traditional story**

### 2. 核心串题思路
> **串联逻辑**：
> 讲述**海南环岛骑行 (Cycling around Hainan Island)** 的经历。
> - **等待**：为了这次骑行，我等待了两个月（准备装备、体能训练）。
> - **兴奋活动**：这是我第一次长途骑行，沿途风景和挑战让我非常兴奋。
> - **外语沟通**：在旅途中遇到外国骑友，第一次用英语与他们交流路线和经验。
> - **传统故事**：在途中听当地人讲了关于**鹿回头 (Luhuitou)** 的美丽传说。

### 3. 通用素材库：海南骑行之旅

**English Answer**

**(Opening)**
I’d like to talk about the time / I waited for my **cycling trip** / around Hainan Island. //
我想讲讲我等待环海南岛骑行之旅的那次经历。

**(Waiting & Excitement)**
It was something / I had been **dreaming of** / for years. / During the two months of preparation, / I felt a mixture of **excitement** and **nervousness**. / When I finally started pedaling along the coast, / the sea breeze and the freedom / made it the most exciting activity I’ve ever done. //
这是我梦想多年的事情。在两个月的准备期间，我感到既兴奋又紧张。当我终于开始沿着海岸骑行时，海风和自由感让这成为我做过的最兴奋的活动。

**(Foreign Language)**
On the second day, / I met a cyclist from Australia / at a rest stop. / It was the first time / I actually used **English** / to have a real conversation / outside of the classroom. / We talked about our routes / and exchanged tips on fixing flat tires. //
第二天，我在一个休息站遇到了一位来自澳大利亚的骑行者。这是我第一次真正用英语在课堂之外进行对话。我们要聊了路线，还交流了修补轮胎的技巧。

**(Traditional Story)**
While in Sanya, / I heard the traditional story of "**Luhuitou**" (The Deer Turning Around). / It’s a romantic legend / about a hunter and a deer / that turned into a beautiful girl. / This story adds a lot of cultural charm / to the beautiful scenery there. //
在三亚时，我听到了“鹿回头”的传统故事。这是一个关于猎人和一只变成美丽女孩的鹿的浪漫传说。这个故事给那里的美景增添了许多文化魅力。
