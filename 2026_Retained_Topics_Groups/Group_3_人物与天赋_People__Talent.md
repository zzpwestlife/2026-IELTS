## Group 3: 人物与天赋 (People & Talent)

### 1. 话题详情 (Topic Details)

#### Topic 1: 钦佩的运动员
> **Describe a sportsperson you admire**
> You should say:
> - Who he/she is
> - What sport he/she plays
> - Why you admire him/her

#### Topic 2: 社交媒体趣事
> **Describe something interesting you saw on social media**
> You should say:
> - What it was
> - When and where you saw it
> - Why you think it was interesting

#### Topic 3: 想提升的天赋
> **Describe a talent you want to improve**
> You should say:
> - What the talent is
> - When you discovered it
> - How you want to improve it
> - And explain why you want to improve it

### 2. 核心串题思路
> **串联逻辑**：
> 以**马龙 (Ma Long)** 或 **罗杰·费德勒 (Roger Federer)** 为核心。
> - **钦佩的运动员**：马龙是乒乓球界的传奇，由于他的全面技术和坚韧精神。
> - **社交媒体趣事**：在微博/Instagram上看到关于马龙比赛的精彩集锦或他在赛后的幽默采访，觉得非常有趣且励志。
> - **想提升的天赋**：受他影响，我想提升自己的**乒乓球技能**（或专注力/体育天赋），希望像他一样在压力下保持冷静。

### 3. 通用素材库：乒乓球传奇马龙

**English Answer**

**(Opening)**
I’d like to talk about **Ma Long**, / who is a legendary **table tennis player** in China. / He is the captain of the Chinese national team / and is known as the "Hexagonal Warrior" / because he is perfect in every aspect of the game. //
我想讲讲马龙，他是中国一位传奇的乒乓球运动员。他是中国国家队的队长，被称为“六边形战士”，因为他在比赛的各个方面都很完美。

**(Social Media Content)**
I saw an interesting video about him / on **Weibo** (a Chinese social media platform). / It was a compilation of his "god-like" shots / followed by a funny interview / where he joked about his age. / It went viral / and got millions of likes. //
我在微博上看到一个关于他的有趣视频。那是他“神级”击球的集锦，接着是一个有趣的采访，他在采访中拿自己的年龄开玩笑。这个视频火了，获得了数百万的点赞。

**(Talent to Improve)**
Watching him play / makes me want to improve my own **table tennis skills**. / I have some basic talent, / but I lack the **consistency** / and mental strength that Ma Long has. / I plan to join a local club / and practice more regularly / to take my skills to the next level. //
看他打球让我想提升自己的乒乓球技术。我有一些基本的天赋，但我缺乏马龙那种稳定性和心理素质。我计划加入一个当地的俱乐部，更规律地练习，把我的技术提升到一个新的水平。
